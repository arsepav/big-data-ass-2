A Dónde Me Lleva La Vida is an album by La Renga, released in 1994. After the independently produced Esquivando Charcos the band published A Dónde Me Lleva La Vida in the same way in November 1993. Four months later, negotiations led to Polygram gaining production and image rights. The multinational is in charge of the distribution of the album and spent 25,000 dollars on the video for "El Rito de Los Corazones Sangrando".
