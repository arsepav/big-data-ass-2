"A House with Love in It" is a popular song composed by Sidney Lippman with lyrics by Sylvia Dee. The song was published in 1956. The recording by The Four Lads (made July 17, 1956) was released by Columbia Records as catalog number 40736. It first reached the Billboard charts on September 15, 1956. On the Disk Jockey chart, it peaked at #20; on the Best Seller chart, at #16; and on the composite chart of the top 100 songs, it reached #23.
