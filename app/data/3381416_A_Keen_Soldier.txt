A Keen Soldier: The Execution of Second World War Private Harold Pringle by Andrew Clark is a historical study of the only execution of a Canadian soldier for military crimes committed during the Second World War. Harold Pringle was executed for murder shortly after the conclusion of the war in Europe.A Keen Soldier - Reviewed by Dr. Steve Lukits Canadian Military Journal. A Keen Soldier was published by Vintage Canada in 2002.
