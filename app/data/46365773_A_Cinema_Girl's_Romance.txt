A Cinema Girl's Romance is a 1915 British silent drama film directed by George Pearson and starring Agnes Glynne, Fred Paul and Alice De Winton. It was based on a novel by Ladbroke Black. The film was made at Isleworth Studios.Harris p.63 ==Cast== * Agnes Glynne as Hazel Wilmot * Fred Paul as Sir Robert Loftus * Alice De Winton as Ruth Roland * Bernard Vaughan * Donald Young ==References== ==Bibliography== * Harris, Ed.
