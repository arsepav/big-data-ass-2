50391667	A Girl from Paris	A Girl from Paris () is a 1954 West German comedy film directed by Franz Seitz, Jr. and starring Etchika Choureau, Erich Schellow, and Hans Leibelt.Dillmann & Loewy p. 71 The film's sets were designed by the art director Arne Flekstad. Location shooting took place in Munich and Paris.
