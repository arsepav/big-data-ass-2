A Day in a Taxi () is a French-language Canadian/French drama film, directed by Robert Ménard and released in 1982.Gerald Pratley, A Century of Canadian Cinema. Lynx Images, 2003. . p. 55. == Plot == A man named Johnny (Gilles Renaud), who becomes the fall guy for a bank robbery, is released from prison on a 36-hour parole. He takes a taxi driven by Michel (Jean Yanne). During the road trip, Johnny has second thoughts and eventually Michel finds himself in the middle. == Recognition == Award Date of ceremony Category Recipient(s) Result Genie Awards March 23, 1983 Best Picture Robert Ménard Jay Scott, "Top Genie prospects for Jack Miner movie".
