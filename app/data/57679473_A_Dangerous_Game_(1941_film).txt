57679473	A Dangerous Game (1941 film)	A Dangerous Game (also known as Who Killed Doc Robbin?) is a 1941 American mystery film directed by John Rawlins and starring Richard Arlen, Andy Devine and Jean Brooks. It is part of Universal Pictures's Aces of Action series."Dangerous Game", A Monthly Film Bulletin; London Vol. 9, Iss. 97, (January 1, 1942): 113. The New York Times called the film "a crack-brained murder mystery.""At the Rialto T.M.P."
