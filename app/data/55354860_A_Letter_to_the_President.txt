55354860	A Letter to the President	A Letter to the President is a 2017 Afghan drama film directed by Roya Sadat. It was selected as the Afghan entry for the Best Foreign Language Film at the 90th Academy Awards, but it was not nominated. ==Plot== Soraya, a low-level government official, is imprisoned when she defends a woman from village lords. Behind bars, she writes the Afghan President for help. ==Cast== * Leena Alam as Soraya * Aziz Deldar as Behzad ==See also== * List of submissions to the 90th Academy Awards for Best Foreign Language Film * List of Afghan submissions for the Academy Award for Best Foreign Language Film ==References== ==External links== * Category:2017 films Category:2017 drama films Category:Dari-language films Category:Afghan drama films
