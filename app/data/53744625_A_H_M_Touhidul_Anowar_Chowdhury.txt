A H M Touhidul Anowar Chowdhury, commonly known as T A Chowdhury, is a Bangladeshi gynaecologist and obstetrician. He was awarded Independence Day Award in medical science in 2017. == Early life and education == Chowdhury was born in 1937. He passed MBBS from Dhaka Medical College in 1960. He achieved FRCS from Royal College of Surgeons of Edinburgh. He obtained MRCOG from Royal College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists in 1965. == Career == He was director of the then IPGMR.
