"A Headache Tomorrow (Or a Heartache Tonight)" is a song written by Chick Rains, and recorded by American country music artist Mickey Gilley. It was released in January 1981 as the second single from the album That's All That Matters to Me. The song was Gilley's eleventh number one hit on the country chart.
