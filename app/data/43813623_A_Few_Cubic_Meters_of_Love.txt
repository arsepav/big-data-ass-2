43813623	A Few Cubic Meters of Love	A Few Cubic Meters of Love (, Chand Metre Moka'ab Eshgh) is a 2014 Iranian- Afghan romance film directed by Jamshid Mahmoudi. It was selected as the Afghan entry for the Best Foreign Language Film at the 87th Academy Awards, but was not nominated. ==Plot== Abdul Salam is an Afghan immigrant.
