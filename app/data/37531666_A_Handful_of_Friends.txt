A Handful of Friends is a 1976 play by David Williamson.A Handful of Friends at AusStage It was written for the South Australian Theatre Company. Williamson said in an interview with Quadrant magazine that he had based one of the characters on Williamson's wife, Kristin, and another on his then- friend Bob Ellis.Michael Connor, "David Williamson by Mrs Williamson" Quadrant 1 July 2009, accessed 6 August 2014 Ellis complained about this in print leading to a public argument between him and Williamson which became notorious. ==References== ==External links== * Category:Plays by David Williamson Category:1976 plays
