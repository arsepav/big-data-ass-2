65783719	A Huntress of Men	A Huntress of Men is a 1916 American silent drama film directed by Lucius J. Henderson and starring Mary Fuller, Joseph W. Girard, and Sidney Bracey. The film was released by Red Feather Photoplays on May 6, 1916. ==Plot== ==Cast== *Mary Fuller as The Huntress *Joseph W.
