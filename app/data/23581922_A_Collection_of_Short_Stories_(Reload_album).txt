A Collection of Short Stories is a 1993 album by Reload (Mark Pritchard and Tom Middleton). The booklet contains an introduction and six short stories written by Dominic Fripp. ==Track listing== Source: Discogs ==Credits== All tracks written, arranged and produced by Mark Pritchard. Tracks 8, 9, 11 co- written by Tom Middleton. Additional production and engineering on tracks 1, 2, 5, 6, 8, 9, 11, 13 by Tom Middleton. "Mosh" engineered by ‘Head’ at The Icehouse.
