A Dangerous Woman is a 1929 American Pre-Code film released by Paramount, based on the Margery Lawrence story, A Woman Who Needed Killing. It was directed by Gerald Grove and Rowland V. Lee from a script by John Farrow and Edward E. Paramore Jr.Staff report (May 14, 1929). Grauman Will Introduce New Color-Talkie. Los Angeles TimesHall, Mordaunt (May 20, 1929).
