56121112	A Glimpse of Paradise	A Glimpse of Paradise is a 1934 British crime film directed by Ralph Ince and starring George Carney, Eve Lister and Wally Patch. It was made as a quota quickie at Teddington Studios by the British subsidiary of Warner Brothers.Wood p.82 It is now considered a lost film. ==Cast== * George Carney as Jim Bogsworth * Eve Lister as Marion Fielding * Robert Cochran as Norman Ware * Wally Patch as Harry * Winifred Oughton as Mrs. Latter * Roddy Hughes as Walter Fielding * Katie Johnson as Mrs. Fielding * Margaret Yarde as Mrs. Kidd * D.
