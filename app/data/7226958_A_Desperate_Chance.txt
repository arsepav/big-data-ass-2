A Desperate Chance is a 1913 American silent short starring Earle Foxe and Alice Hollister. Directed by Kenean Buel, the drama features the same cast and crew of the film that preceded it that year, A Sawmill Hazard. ==Cast== *Alice Hollister *Earle Foxe *Helen Lindroth *Robert G.
