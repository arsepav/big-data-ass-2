A Daughter of the Law is a 1921 American silent crime film directed by Jack Conway and starring Carmel Myers, John B. O'Brien and Fred Kohler.Parish & Pitts p.176 ==Cast== * Carmel Myers as Nora Hayes * John B. O'Brien as Jim Garth * Fred Kohler as George Stacey * Jack Walters as Slim Dolan * Dick La Reno as Pata Marlowe * Charles Arling as Insp. Hayes * Joseph Bennett as Eddie Hayes ==References== ==Bibliography== * James Robert Parish & Michael R. Pitts. Film directors: a guide to their American films.
