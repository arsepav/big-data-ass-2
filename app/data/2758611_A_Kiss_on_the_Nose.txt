A Kiss on the Nose is a 2004 American short film that premiered in the United States on August 11, 2004 at the Rhode Island International Film Festival. Written and directed by Laura Neri, the film stars Azita Ghanizada as a woman looking back on her relationship with her recently deceased father, played by Gianfranco Russo.
