A Family Affair is an upcoming American romantic comedy film directed by Richard LaGravenese and written by Carrie Solomon. The film stars Nicole Kidman, Zac Efron, Joey King, Liza Koshy, and Kathy Bates. A Family Affair is scheduled to be released on November 17, 2023, by Netflix. ==Premise== A young woman named Zara works as an assistant for movie star Chris Cole.
